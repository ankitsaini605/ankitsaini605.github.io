Practice Questions with Solutions

Question 1:

A number when divided by 5 leaves a remainder 2. What will be the remainder when the square of that number is divided by 5?

Solution:

Let number be (5k + 2). Square = (5k + 2)2 = 25k2 + 20k + 4.

When divided by 5, remainder = 4.

Question 2:

The ratio of the ages of A and B is 3:4. Five years hence, the ratio will be 4:5. Find their present ages.

Solution:

Let ages be 3x and 4x.

(3x + 5)/(4x + 5) = 4/5 → cross-multiply and solve:

5(3x + 5) = 4(4x + 5)

15x + 25 = 16x + 20 → x = 5.

Ages: 15 and 20 years.

Question 3:

A person covers 1/3rd of a distance at 30 km/h, the next 1/3rd at 40 km/h, and the last 1/3rd at 60 km/h. What is the average speed?

Solution:

Let distance = D → each part = D/3.

Time = (D/3)/30 + (D/3)/40 + (D/3)/60 = D(1/90 + 1/120 + 1/180).

Total time = D × (8/360) = D/45.

Average speed = D / (D/45) = 45 km/h.
