Best Morning Routine for Students

  1. Wake Up Early: Between 5:30 AM – 6:00 AM
  2. Hydrate: Drink water before phone
  3. 20 Min Exercise: Boosts blood flow to brain
  4. 30 Min Study: Pick light revision topic
  5. Healthy Breakfast: Banana + Milk + Dry Fruits
  6. No Mobile till 8 AM: Mind stays fresh

Consistency is key — turn this into habit and feel the difference in 7 days!
