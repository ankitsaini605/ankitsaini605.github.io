📊 Ratio & Proportion – Concepts & Tricks

🔹 Formula

If a:b = 2:3 and b:c = 4:5, then a:b:c = 8:12:15

🧮 Example

A:B = 3:4, B:C = 5:6 → Multiply cross B: 3×5 = 15, 4×5=20, 4×6=24 → A:B:C = 15:20:24

⚡ Shortcut

Always cross multiply middle term if chained ratios are given.
