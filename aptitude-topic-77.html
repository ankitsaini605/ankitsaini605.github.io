Topic : Clock Problems

Solve time-based questions involving angles between clock hands, mirror images, and time loss or gain.

Practice Questions

  1. Q1. At what time between 2 and 3 o’clock will the hands of a clock be together?
  2. Q2. Find the angle between the hands of a clock at 3:15.
  3. Q3. How many times in a day do the hands of a clock coincide?

Answers & Explanations

  • A1. Hands coincide every 65.45 minutes → Answer: Approximately 2:10:55
  • A2. Formula: |30H - (11/2)M| → |30×3 - 11/2×15| = 7.5°
  • A3. Hands coincide 22 times in 24 hours

Pro Tip

Use the formula: Angle = |30×H − (11/2)×M| for quick calculations.
