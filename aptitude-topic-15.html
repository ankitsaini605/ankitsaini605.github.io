Aptitude Topic 15 - Number System

Number system is the base of quantitative aptitude. It covers types of numbers, divisibility rules, remainders, cyclicity, and various other concepts that frequently appear in competitive exams.

Types of Numbers

  • Natural Numbers
  • Whole Numbers
  • Integers
  • Rational Numbers
  • Irrational Numbers
  • Real Numbers

Divisibility Rules

Learn divisibility rules for numbers like 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, 11, etc. These help in solving many aptitude questions quickly.

Remainder Theorem

Concept of finding the remainder when a large number is divided by another number using modular arithmetic.

Cyclicity

Cyclicity of digits in powers, useful for solving problems involving last digits of large powers.

Example Problem

Question: Find the remainder when 2100 is divided by 7.

Solution: The cyclicity of 2 mod 7 is 3. The remainder is 4.

Tips & Tricks

  • Memorize basic divisibility rules.
  • Practice remainder and cyclicity questions.
  • Use modular arithmetic to simplify complex problems.

Practice Questions

  1. Find the remainder when 597 is divided by 13.
  2. How many 3-digit numbers are divisible by 7?
  3. Find the sum of digits of 250.
