📚 What is Ratio?

Ratio is a way to compare two quantities by division. If two quantities are 'a' and 'b', then the ratio of a to b is a:b or a/b.

⚙️ What is Proportion?

When two ratios are equal, they are said to be in proportion. For example, if a/b = c/d, then a, b, c, d are in proportion.

✨ Important Formulas:

  • If a:b = c:d, then ad = bc.
  • Compound Ratio of (a:b) and (c:d) = (ac):(bd).
  • Inverse Ratio of a:b = b:a.

📝 Example Problems:

Example 1:

Divide ₹1800 between A and B in the ratio 3:2.

Solution: Total parts = 3+2 = 5
A's share = (3/5)×1800 = ₹1080
B's share = (2/5)×1800 = ₹720

Example 2:

If 5 pencils cost ₹25, how much do 8 pencils cost?

Solution: Cost of 1 pencil = ₹25 / 5 = ₹5
Cost of 8 pencils = ₹5 × 8 = ₹40

📌 Tips for Solving Ratio & Proportion Questions:

  • Understand the basic concept of ratios.
  • Always express ratios in their simplest form.
  • Cross multiplication is the key technique in proportions.
