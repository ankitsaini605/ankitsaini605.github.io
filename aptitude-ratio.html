Ratio and Proportion – Concept + Quiz

Concept: Ratio is a comparison of two quantities. If A:B = 2:3, it means A is 2 parts and B is 3 parts.

  1. Q: If 3x = 4y, then what is the ratio of x to y?
    Ans: 4:3
  2. Q: A and B earn in the ratio 3:5. If A earns ₹12000, how much does B earn?
    Ans: ₹20000
  3. Q: The ratio of boys to girls in a class is 4:5. If there are 36 boys, how many girls are there?
    Ans: 45
