Aptitude Topic 52: Practice Problems & Solutions

Enhance your aptitude skills with these carefully selected problems and detailed step-by-step solutions.

Problem 1:

A train 120 meters long is running at a speed of 60 km/h. How much time will it take to cross a bridge 180 meters long?
Solution:
Speed = 60 km/h = (60 × 1000) / 3600 = 16.67 m/s
Distance to cover = length of train + length of bridge = 120 + 180 = 300 meters
Time = Distance / Speed = 300 / 16.67 ≈ 18 seconds
Answer: Approximately 18 seconds

Problem 2:

A rectangular garden is 50 meters long and 30 meters wide. Find the cost of fencing the garden at ₹20 per meter.
Solution:
Perimeter of rectangle = 2 × (length + breadth) = 2 × (50 + 30) = 160 meters
Cost of fencing = Perimeter × rate = 160 × 20 = ₹3200
Answer: ₹3200

Problem 3:

Simplify: (3/4) ÷ (5/8) + (7/10) × (2/3)
Solution:
(3/4) ÷ (5/8) = (3/4) × (8/5) = 24/20 = 6/5 = 1.2
(7/10) × (2/3) = 14/30 = 7/15 ≈ 0.4667
Sum = 1.2 + 0.4667 = 1.6667 = 5/3
Answer: 5/3 or 1.6667
