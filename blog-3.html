5 Exam Hacks Toppers Use Daily

  • Smart Revision: Focus on weak topics in morning.
  • 90/10 Rule: 90% effort on 10% most important chapters.
  • Timer Based Study: Set 25-min slots with 5-min break.
  • Question Practice Daily: 10-20 questions per subject.
  • Digital Detox: Zero phone during main study hours.
