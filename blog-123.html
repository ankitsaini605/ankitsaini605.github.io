📚 30-Day Study Challenge

📅 Day-Wise Plan

  • Day 1–5: Learn basic concepts (1 subject daily)
  • Day 6–10: Solve 15 MCQs daily from all topics
  • Day 11–20: Mix practice + 2-hour mock tests
  • Day 21–30: Revise mistakes, attempt new mocks

🧠 Rules

  • No distractions for 2 hours morning
  • Record daily learning in a log
  • Take Sunday as quiz & feedback day

✅ Benefits

  • Focus improvement
  • Habit building
  • Strong revision base
