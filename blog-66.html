Ultimate Study Blueprint for Toppers

📌 Topic 1: Pomodoro Technique

This method breaks your study time into 25-minute focused sessions followed by a 5-minute break. It's effective for improving concentration and stamina.

📌 Topic 2: Active Recall

Instead of re-reading, ask yourself questions and write answers from memory. This boosts long-term retention.

📌 Topic 3: Spaced Repetition

Review content at increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 7 days) to fight forgetting curve. Use apps like Anki for this.

📌 Topic 4: Morning vs Night Study

  • Morning: Best for problem-solving, fresh memory.
  • Night: Best for revision, concept clarity, less distraction.
