Topic 7: Percentage Problems

Test your understanding of percentage concepts. Ideal for government exams, interviews, and campus placements.

Practice Questions

  1. Q1. If a number is increased by 20%, it becomes 144. What is the original number?
  2. Q2. A's salary is 25% more than B’s. If B’s salary is ₹40,000, what is A’s salary?
  3. Q3. A number is decreased by 10% and then increased by 10%. What is the net change?

Answers & Explanations

  • A1. 144 ÷ 1.2 = 120
  • A2. ₹40,000 × 1.25 = ₹50,000
  • A3. Net change = (1 - 0.1) × (1 + 0.1) = 0.99 → 1% decrease
