Aptitude Topic 104 - Time, Speed & Distance Problems

Welcome to Aptitude Topic 104. Here you will practice a wide variety of questions based on Time, Speed, and Distance. These are important topics for various competitive exams.

Concepts Covered

  • Basic Speed-Distance-Time Relationship
  • Relative Speed
  • Boats & Streams
  • Trains Problems
  • Conversions between units

Practice Questions with Solutions

Question 1:

A car travels 300 km at a speed of 75 km/h. How much time will it take to cover this distance?

Solution:

Time = Distance / Speed = 300 / 75 = 4 hours.

Question 2:

A train 120 meters long is running at a speed of 54 km/h. How long will it take to cross a platform 180 meters long?

Solution:

Speed = 54 km/h = 54 * 1000 / 3600 = 15 m/s.

Total distance = 120 + 180 = 300 meters.

Time = 300 / 15 = 20 seconds.

Question 3:

A man rows downstream at 8 km/h and upstream at 5 km/h. What is the speed of the stream?

Solution:

Speed of stream = (Downstream - Upstream) / 2 = (8 - 5) / 2 = 1.5 km/h.

Expert Tips

  • Always remember: Speed = Distance / Time.
  • When units are different (km/h and m/s), convert accordingly.
  • For Boats & Streams, understand the difference between downstream and upstream.
  • In Train problems, add the lengths correctly when crossing objects.
