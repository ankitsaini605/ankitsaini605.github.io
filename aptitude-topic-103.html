Practice Questions with Solutions

Question 1:

A can do a piece of work in 12 days and B can do the same work in 16 days. If they work together for 4 days, how much work is left?

Solution:

A's 1 day work = 1/12, B's 1 day work = 1/16.

Combined 1 day work = (1/12 + 1/16) = (4 + 3) / 48 = 7/48.

Work done in 4 days = (7/48) * 4 = 28/48 = 7/12.

Work left = 1 - 7/12 = 5/12.

Question 2:

If ₹12,000 amounts to ₹15,876 in 3 years at compound interest, what is the rate of interest?

Solution:

A = P(1 + r/100)n.

15,876 = 12,000(1 + r/100)3.

(1 + r/100)3 = 15,876 / 12,000 = 1.323.

Taking cube root: 1 + r/100 = 1.10 → r = 10%.

Question 3:

The ratio of the number of boys to girls in a school is 3:5. If there are 1200 students, how many girls are there?

Solution:

Total parts = 3 + 5 = 8.

1 part = 1200 / 8 = 150.

Girls = 5 parts = 5 * 150 = 750 girls.

Expert Tips
