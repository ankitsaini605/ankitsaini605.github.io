Aptitude Topic 56: Advanced Problems on Percentages

Master complex percentage problems with stepwise explanations to improve your quantitative aptitude skills.

Problem 1:

The price of a product was increased by 20% and then decreased by 15%. What is the net percentage change in the price?
Solution:
Let original price = 100 units
After 20% increase = 100 + 20% of 100 = 120 units
After 15% decrease = 120 - 15% of 120 = 120 - 18 = 102 units
Net change = 102 - 100 = 2 units increase
Net percentage change = (2 / 100) * 100 = 2% increase
Answer: 2% increase

Problem 2:

A shopkeeper sells an article at 10% profit. If he had bought it at 20% less and sold at 5% profit, what would have been the selling price?
Solution:
Let cost price = Rs. 100
Selling price at 10% profit = 100 + 10% of 100 = Rs. 110
New cost price = 20% less = 100 - 20 = Rs. 80
New selling price at 5% profit = 80 + 5% of 80 = 80 + 4 = Rs. 84
Answer: Rs. 84

Problem 3:

A sum of money is divided among A, B, and C in the ratio 3:4:5. If A gets Rs. 600 more than B, find the total sum.
Solution:
Ratio difference between A and B = 4 - 3 = 1 part
1 part = Rs. 600
Total parts = 3 + 4 + 5 = 12 parts
Total sum = 12 * 600 = Rs. 7200
Answer: Rs. 7200
