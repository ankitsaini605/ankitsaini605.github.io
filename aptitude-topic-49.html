Aptitude Topic 49: Practice Problems & Solutions

Problem 1:

A boat travels 30 km downstream in 2 hours and returns upstream in 3 hours. Find the speed of the boat in still water and the speed of the stream.
Solution:
Let speed of boat in still water = b km/hr
Speed of stream = s km/hr
Downstream speed = b + s = 30/2 = 15 km/hr
Upstream speed = b - s = 30/3 = 10 km/hr
Adding: 2b = 25 => b = 12.5 km/hr
Subtracting: 2s = 5 => s = 2.5 km/hr
Answer: Boat speed = 12.5 km/hr, Stream speed = 2.5 km/hr

Problem 2:

Two pipes can fill a tank in 12 hours and 15 hours respectively. Both pipes are opened together but the second pipe is closed after 4 hours. How long will the tank take to fill completely?
Solution:
Rate of pipe 1 = 1/12 per hour
Rate of pipe 2 = 1/15 per hour
Both open for 4 hours: Work done = 4 × (1/12 + 1/15) = 4 × (5/60 + 4/60) = 4 × 9/60 = 3/5
Remaining work = 1 - 3/5 = 2/5
Pipe 1 alone fills remaining: Time = (2/5) ÷ (1/12) = (2/5) × 12 = 4.8 hours
Total time = 4 + 4.8 = 8.8 hours
Answer: 8.8 hours (or 8 hours 48 minutes)

Problem 3:

In how many ways can 5 boys and 4 girls be seated in a row such that all the girls sit together?
Solution:
Treat 4 girls as one unit.
Number of units = 5 boys + 1 group = 6
Number of ways to arrange these units = 6!
Number of ways to arrange 4 girls inside their group = 4!
Total ways = 6! × 4! = 720 × 24 = 17280
Answer: 17,280 ways
