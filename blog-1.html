How to Study for 6 Hours Without Losing Focus

  1. Pomodoro Technique: 25 min study + 5 min break
  2. Clear Distractions: Keep phone away, silent mode
  3. Use Timetable: Divide 6 hours into subjects
  4. Hydrate & Snack: Light food keeps brain active
  5. Mindset: Study as a mission, not punishment

Keep your desk clean and your goals clear. Stay consistent!
