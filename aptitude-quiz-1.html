Time & Work - MCQ Set 1

  1. Q: A can do a piece of work in 20 days. B can do it in 30 days. In how many days will they complete it together?
    Ans: 12 days
  2. Q: If 12 men can complete a work in 15 days, how many men are required to complete it in 10 days?
    Ans: 18 men
  3. Q: If A is twice as good a workman as B, then working together they can finish a work in 12 days. In how many days will A alone finish the work?
    Ans: 18 days
