Topper Study Formula – Plan Your Day

📌 Time Blocking

Divide day into 2–3 hour subject blocks with breaks. Example: 9-11 Physics, 11:30-1 Maths.

🧠 Morning Routine

  • Wake up by 6 AM
  • Drink water, stretch, read goals aloud
  • Start 90 min deep study block

📅 Weekly Reset

Every Sunday: review wins, what didn’t work, and plan micro-goals for next 7 days.
