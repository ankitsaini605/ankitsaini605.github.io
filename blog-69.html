Blog #69: Boost Your Memory & Focus

🔍 Topic: Revision Strategy

Use the 1-3-7 formula: Revise after 1 day, 3 days, and 7 days for long-term retention.

📚 Topic: Nighttime Study Tips

  • Revise already-studied concepts only
  • Don’t learn new material before sleep
  • Summarize the day on paper

🛏️ Bonus Tip: Sleep + Review

10 minutes of reading before sleep can lock learning into long-term memory.
