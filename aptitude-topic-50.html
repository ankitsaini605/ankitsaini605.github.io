Aptitude Topic 50: Practice Problems & Solutions

Enhance your aptitude skills with these carefully selected problems and their step-by-step solutions for Topic 50.

Problem 1:

A train 120 meters long is running at a speed of 60 km/h. How long will it take to pass a man standing near the track?
Solution:
Speed = 60 km/h = (60 × 1000)/3600 = 50/3 m/s
Length of train = 120 m
Time = Distance / Speed = 120 / (50/3) = 120 × 3 / 50 = 7.2 seconds
Answer: 7.2 seconds

Problem 2:

If the sum of the ages of a father and son is 50 years and the father's age is three times the son's age, find their present ages.
Solution:
Let son's age = x
Father's age = 3x
x + 3x = 50
4x = 50 => x = 12.5
Father's age = 3 × 12.5 = 37.5
Answer: Son = 12.5 years, Father = 37.5 years

Problem 3:

How many different 4-digit numbers can be formed using digits 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 if repetition is not allowed?
Solution:
Number of digits = 5
Number of 4-digit numbers without repetition = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 = 120
Answer: 120 numbers
