Aptitude Topic 123 - Boats and Streams

Welcome to Aptitude Topic 123. In this section, you will learn about Boats and Streams — a key topic in time, speed, and distance-based aptitude questions.

Key Concepts & Formulas

  • Speed of boat in still water = b km/h
  • Speed of stream = s km/h
  • Downstream speed = (b + s) km/h
  • Upstream speed = (b - s) km/h
  • Time = Distance / Speed

Practice Questions with Solutions

Question 1:

A boat can travel 30 km downstream in 2 hours. The speed of the stream is 4 km/h. Find the speed of the boat in still water.

Solution:

Downstream speed = 30/2 = 15 km/h
b + 4 = 15 → b = 11 km/h

Question 2:

A boat can go 40 km upstream in 5 hours. The speed of the stream is 3 km/h. Find the speed of the boat in still water.

Solution:

Upstream speed = 40/5 = 8 km/h
b - 3 = 8 → b = 11 km/h

Question 3:

A man can row 12 km downstream in 1.5 hours and 6 km upstream in 1.5 hours. Find the speed of the boat in still water and the speed of the stream.

Solution:

Downstream speed = 12/1.5 = 8 km/h
Upstream speed = 6/1.5 = 4 km/h
b = (8 + 4)/2 = 6 km/h
s = (8 - 4)/2 = 2 km/h

Tips & Tricks

  • Always clearly identify upstream and downstream conditions.
  • Use the basic formula: Time = Distance / Speed.
  • For combined distance and time problems, focus on relative speeds.
  • Practice questions of increasing difficulty to build confidence.
