Topic 76: Calendar

Learn to solve problems related to days of the week, leap years, odd days and calendar date calculations.

Practice Questions

  1. Q1. What day of the week was 26th January 1950?
  2. Q2. How many odd days are there in 200 years?
  3. Q3. Which year after 2024 will have the same calendar as 2024?

Answers & Explanations

  • A1. Use odd day calculation → Answer: Thursday
  • A2. 200 years = (200 ÷ 100 × 5) odd days = 10 = 3 odd days
  • A3. Next same calendar → Leap year after 2024 = 2052

Pro Tip

Remember: A leap year has 2 odd days, and a normal year has 1 odd day. Use this in backward/forward calendar questions.
