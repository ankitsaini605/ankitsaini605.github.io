Topic 78: Calendar Problems

Master how to calculate days, odd days, leap years, and find the day of any given date.

Practice Questions

  1. Q1. What day of the week was 15th August 1947?
  2. Q2. Which year will have the same calendar as 2024?
  3. Q3. How many odd days are there in 400 years?

Answers & Explanations

  • A1. Use odd days method → Friday
  • A2. Same calendar repeats after 6, 11, or 28 years → Answer: 2052
  • A3. 400 years = 0 odd days

Shortcut Trick

Every 400 years, the calendar repeats exactly because it has 0 odd days.
