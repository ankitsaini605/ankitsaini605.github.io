Topic 75: Clocks

This topic deals with problems related to angles formed by hands of the clock, and timing calculations based on clocks.

Practice Questions

  1. Q1. What is the angle between the hands of a clock at 3:15?
  2. Q2. At what time between 4 and 5 o'clock will the hands of the clock be at right angles?
  3. Q3. How many times in a day do the hands of a clock coincide?

Answers & Explanations

  • A1. Minute hand at 90°, Hour hand at 97.5° → Angle = |90 - 97.5| = 7.5°
  • A2. Use clock formulas for right angles → Answer = 32 8/11 minutes past 4
  • A3. Hands coincide 22 times in 24 hours

Expert Tip

Angle between hands = |30H - (11/2)M| is the key formula. Use it wisely in every clock question.
