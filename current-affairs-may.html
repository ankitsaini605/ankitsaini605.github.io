Here is the most important General Knowledge and Current Affairs of May 2025. These updates are especially curated for students preparing for government exams.

✅ Highlights – May 2025

  • May 1: Labour Day celebrated across India with various rallies & awareness campaigns.
  • May 5: India successfully tests next-gen Agni-VI missile.
  • May 9: Supreme Court rules in favor of uniform civil code debate.
  • May 12: ISRO launches 5 satellites under PSLV-C66 mission.
  • May 15: RBI keeps repo rate unchanged at 6.50%.
  • May 20: Cyclone 'Kavach' hits the eastern coast; NDRF active in Odisha.
  • May 23: Indian team wins Asia Cup Hockey Championship 2025.
  • May 28: Government announces ₹12,000 crore EV subsidy plan under FAME 3.0.
