5 Quick Tips to Crack Competitive Exams

  1. Set Daily Targets: Break syllabus into chunks.
  2. Revise Smart: Don’t just read — make notes!
  3. Practice MCQs: Daily 20–30 practice questions.
  4. Stay Healthy: Sleep well, eat light.
  5. Mock Tests: Weekly mocks like real exam.
