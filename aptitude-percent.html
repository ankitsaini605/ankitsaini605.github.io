Percentage – Concept & Quiz

Percentage is a number or ratio expressed as a fraction of 100.

  1. Q: What is 25% of 200?
    Ans: 50
  2. Q: A number is increased by 20% and then decreased by 20%. What is the net change?
    Ans: 4% decrease
  3. Q: If 60% of students passed, how many failed out of 500?
    Ans: 200
