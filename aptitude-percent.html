🎯 Percentage - Concepts

Percentage is one of the most frequently used topics in Aptitude and everyday life. It is widely used in topics like Profit & Loss, Discounts, Data Interpretation, etc.

👉 Formula:

  • Percentage (%) = (Value / Total Value) × 100
  • Increase/Decrease % = (Change / Original Value) × 100

📝 Tips & Tricks

  • Always convert percentage to fraction while solving ratio-based problems.
  • Memorize common percentage-to-fraction conversions (eg. 50% = 1/2, 25% = 1/4, 20% = 1/5, etc.).
  • Be careful when applying successive percentage changes (use formula: Final % = x + y + (xy/100)).

🧮 Practice Problems

  1. The price of a commodity increased by 25%. By what percent must a consumer reduce his consumption so that his expenditure remains the same?
  2. If A’s income is 20% more than B’s, by what % is B’s income less than A’s?
  3. In an examination, 75% of students passed in Math, 60% passed in English and 50% passed in both. Find % of students who passed in at least one subject.
  4. If the population of a town increases by 10% annually, what will be the population after 2 years if the current population is 50,000?
