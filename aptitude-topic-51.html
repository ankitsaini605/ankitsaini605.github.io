Aptitude Topic 51: Practice Problems & Solutions

Sharpen your aptitude skills with these important problems and detailed solutions for Topic 51.

Problem 1:

A shopkeeper sells an article for ₹500 with a profit of 20%. What is the cost price of the article?
Solution:
Let cost price = ₹x
Profit = 20% of cost price = 0.20x
Selling price = cost price + profit = x + 0.20x = 1.20x
Given selling price = ₹500
So, 1.20x = 500 => x = 500 / 1.20 = ₹416.67
Answer: ₹416.67

Problem 2:

A cylindrical tank has a radius of 7 m and height of 10 m. Find its volume. (Use π = 22/7)
Solution:
Volume of cylinder = πr²h = (22/7) × 7² × 10 = (22/7) × 49 × 10 = 22 × 7 × 10 = 1540 m³
Answer: 1540 cubic meters

Problem 3:

Find the LCM and HCF of 36 and 60.
Solution:
Prime factors:
36 = 2² × 3²
60 = 2² × 3 × 5

HCF = product of common prime factors with least powers = 2² × 3 = 4 × 3 = 12
LCM = product of all prime factors with highest powers = 2² × 3² × 5 = 4 × 9 × 5 = 180
Answer: HCF = 12, LCM = 180
