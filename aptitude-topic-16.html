Aptitude Topic 16 - Simplification

Simplification questions test your ability to quickly and accurately solve arithmetic expressions. These questions often appear in bank exams, SSC, and other competitive exams.

Key Concepts

  • BODMAS Rule (Bracket, Order, Division, Multiplication, Addition, Subtraction)
  • Squares and Square Roots
  • Cubes and Cube Roots
  • Percentage Conversions
  • Fractions and Decimals

Common Shortcuts

  • Approximation techniques to eliminate options.
  • Memorizing squares and cubes of numbers up to 30.
  • Using Vedic Math tricks for faster calculations.

Example Problem

Question: Simplify: (25 × 16) ÷ (4 × 5)

Solution: (400) ÷ (20) = 20

Tips & Tricks

  • Always follow the BODMAS rule to avoid mistakes.
  • Learn percentage-to-fraction conversions for faster calculations.
  • Practice mental math daily to improve speed.

Practice Questions

  1. Simplify: (45 × 12) ÷ (9 × 5)
  2. Find the value of √625 + √81.
  3. Simplify: (56% of 500) + (75% of 400).
