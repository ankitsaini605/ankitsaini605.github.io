🧠 Deep Work for Toppers

📌 What is Deep Work?

Deep work is the ability to focus without distraction on cognitively demanding tasks. It improves retention and output.

🧠 Why It Works

  • Eliminates distractions
  • Strengthens focus
  • Enhances learning speed

🔒 How to Practice

  1. Turn off notifications
  2. Use a dedicated quiet place
  3. Do 90-minute sessions

📘 Real Toppers' Tips

Many toppers use "Time Block Calendar" + Deep Work combo to finish syllabus 2x faster.

🎯 Result

More clarity, less stress, long-term retention
