Aptitude Concepts – Percentages, Interest

📘 Percentages

Formula: (Value × Percentage) ÷ 100

Example: 25% of 480 = (480 × 25)/100 = 120

📘 Simple Interest

Formula: SI = (P × R × T)/100

Q: P=5000, R=6%, T=2 years → SI = ₹600

📘 Compound Interest

Formula: A = P(1 + R/100)^T

Q: P=5000, R=10%, T=2 → A = 5000×1.1×1.1 = ₹6050
