Topic 70: Pipes and Cisterns

Solve problems related to filling and emptying tanks using pipes. These types of questions are common in aptitude tests.

Practice Questions

  1. Q1. Pipe A can fill a tank in 6 hours. Pipe B can fill it in 4 hours. How long will they take together?
  2. Q2. Pipe A can fill a tank in 5 hours, and pipe B can empty it in 10 hours. If both are opened together, how long will the tank take to fill?
  3. Q3. A pipe fills a tank in 3 hours. A second pipe fills it in 6 hours, but a third pipe empties it in 2 hours. If all are opened, how long to fill the tank?

Answers & Explanations

  • A1. 1/6 + 1/4 = 5/12 → Time = 12/5 = 2.4 hours
  • A2. 1/5 - 1/10 = 1/10 → Time = 10 hours
  • A3. 1/3 + 1/6 - 1/2 = (2+1-3)/6 = 0 → Tank will never fill

Expert Tip

Convert the rates to “per hour” and apply addition or subtraction based on whether the pipe fills or empties.
