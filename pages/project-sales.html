Case study

Sales insights: cohorts, RFM, and forecasting

A 24‑month transactional dataset turned into decisions: which customers to retain, which categories to push, and what to expect next quarter.

Executive summary

  • Lift levers: Category mix and targeted discounts to high‑value “Champions” improved retention by 6–9%.
  • Forecast: Seasonal peaks at month‑ends; smoothing inventory around those windows reduces stockouts.
  • Churn risk: “At risk” and “Hibernating” customers respond to win‑back email within 7–10 days.

Monthly revenue

Time series shows overall growth with monthly spikes aligned to campaigns and salary cycles.

RFM segmentation

Segments like “Champions” and “Loyal” carry disproportionate revenue; “At risk” deserve targeted nurture.

Cohort retention

Retention cohorts normalize for acquisition timing and show the impact of lifecycle messaging.

Method

Data cleaning (outliers, missing values) → EDA → RFM (quantiles) → cohort construction → SARIMAX/LGBM exploration (offline) → precompute JSON for the site. All interactive charts are rendered client‑side for speed.
